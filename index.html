Becoming a poet

If someone would have told me two years ago that I would be able to lay down my skin into words so comfortably, I would not have believed them, for I was a shy 19 year old.

Glancing upon the last two years of my life it would be appropriate for me to quote Robert Frost by saying
"Two roads diverged into the woods,and I,
I took the one less travelled by,
and that has made all the difference"
as making hard choices has brought where i am right now
